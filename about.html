About the Designer

email: denise.blankenberger@gmail.com

instagram: @denisemberger

Currently, I am a first year graduate student at the University of Oregon. I completed my BS in Architecture degree with a minor in sustainability at Ball State University in 2016. However, my hometown is Evansville, IN, and I am a midwestern girl at heart. This field of study is my passion; I am always looking for new opportunities to push the limits and understanding of architecture through innovative design.

I anticipate the intersection of my interests becoming a critical focus for how to bring responsive design into everyday life.

When I'm not in studio or posted up at my laptop, you can find me running for miles, practicing yoga, or watercolor painting.
