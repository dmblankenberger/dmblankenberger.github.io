Allegre

Student-run restaurant website redesign

I was junior project manager on the redesign of a website for Allegre, a student-run teaching restaurant at Ball State University. My team consisted of three designers and a developer working together to rebrand and redesign their website, primarily for reserving tables to dine at the restaurant.

In addition to the reservation functionality, the focus of the website was on displaying the meals in an elegant way. The resulting design uses accordion-style stacked menu listings that expand to show the meal courses and nutrition, respectively, when clicked. Previous meals are also displayed, but in black and white to distinguish between current and past.

The meal managers page highlights the student chefs that create the meals. Clicking on an image expands the bio on the chef(s).
