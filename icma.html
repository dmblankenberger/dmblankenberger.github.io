Interplay

Interplay: a center for discovery


The Interplay concept serves to display the full potential of concrete masonry. From the sloped corridor wall that unites the two sites together to the uniquely colored and textured double-withe CMU walls of the rest of the facility, each space is thoroughly analyzed to exhibit the connection between right and left brain thinking.






As the facility is an art and craft center, the concrete masonry units are used in a way that identifies individual functions and highlights the types of learning to be experienced within each space. For example, the spaces defined by blue, polished CMU's have a reflective finish to relate to the areas defined as "introspective" and require quiet contemplation. Such spaces include the library and media reference centers, and personal living quarters.
